What are the options for doing this?

What are their pros and cons with respect to best practices?
Does any one of them have a decisive advantage or a
disqualifying limitation?

Let’s think step by step and check our work to make sure we get
the right answer. If this involves a codebase or repository, ground
the options and pros/cons in the actual repo state and cite
`file:line`; otherwise cite or quote reputable sources, so we know
it is grounded.
